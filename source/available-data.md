The following datasets are currently available via the EcoData Retriever.

* [North American Breeding Bird Survey (both 10 and 50 stop)](https://www.pwrc.usgs.gov/BBS/index.cfm?CFID=1739787&CFTOKEN=c4e129d121bc7811-67E3F6EA-A32F-D934-5A2E9592322BC3DC)
* [Forest Inventory & Analysis](http://www.fia.fs.fed.us/)
* [Alwyn Gentry's Forest Transect Data](http://www.wlbcenter.org/gentry_data.htm)
* [Species composition and abundance of mammalian communities](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E092/201/)
* [National Phenology Network](https://www.usanpn.org/)
* [Plant Taxonomy - USDA](http://www.plants.usda.gov/classification.html)
* [Life history characteristics of placental non-volant mammals](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E084/093/)
* [Phylogeny and metabolic rates in mammals](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E091/198/)
* [Predator and prey body sizes in marine food webs](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E089/051/)
* [Avian body sizes in relation to fecundity, mating system, display behavior, and resource sharing](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E088/096/)
* [Long-term monitoring and experimental manipulation of a Chihuahuan Desert ecosystem](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E090/118/)
* [Long-term mapped quadrats from Kansas prairie: demographic information for herbaceous plants](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E088/161/)
* [Tree growth, mortality, physical condition, and microsite in an old-growth lowland tropical rain forest](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E087/132/)
* [Thirty years of permanent vegetation plots, Mount St. Helens, Washington](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E091/152/)
* [A 12-year study on the scaling of vascular plant composition in an Oklahoma tallgrass prairie](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E091/124/)
* [Body mass of late Quaternary mammals](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E084/094/)
* [PanTHERIA: a species-level database of life history, ecology, and geography mammals](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E090/184/)
* [Densities and cover data for intertidal organisms in the Gulf of Maine, USA, from 2003 to 2007](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E089/032/)
* [Forest stand structure and composition along environmental gradients in the central Western Ghats of India](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E091/216/)
* [Multi-decade, spatially explicit population studies of canopy dynamics in Michigan old-growth forests](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E090/251/)
* [Mapped quadrats in sagebrush steppe: long-term data for analyzing demographic rates and interactions](http://esapubs.org/archive/ecol/E091/243/)
